About the past history in Doculate

This world was nothing at first, but the gods created the lands, water and even us, also the animals, on this world, everyone need to be strong to protect themselves, strong things eat the weak things, the weak things eat more weak things, the world works like that and everyone already sick of that world pattern in the world, once, the early humans try to fight the beasts that often destroy their happy Afternoon life, they carried spears, try to throw at them, but it all didn’t work at all. Thus, they were all ate by the beasts, before the big war between the nature and human beings, the gods made predictions about the wars and which species will finally survive, several gods made predictions on human beings, because they were the smartest in the species, but the big error that they make really frustrated the gods and their consequences of lost is they might not be the leader of the whole heaven(as they believed that the gods were appeared and lived in heaven, or accurately, on heaven) or even death, they don’t want to lost so easily, so they gave the human beings magic and armors, if they don’t have the match, the history will go to another direction. As the god thought, human beings won the big match Below the heaven and the gods won the match on the heaven. The gods that voted for the other species were sentenced to the world to be either a human (which is not bad) or an animal. But, human beings were too proud and they made most things no matter with the gods, and more and more scientist appeared and encourage other people to believe science instead of gods and religions. That made the gods super mad and finally gave the divine punishment.

Divine punishment.

There is seven god that stand for their country, they are kind god, hope god, thankful god, friend god, beauty god, sadness god, and calm god. They all controlled a part of the old Doculate, , people just have big walls that border out the border for different “countries”. They lived happily, even though the other species often bothered them. But, when the gods find out that religious people is decreasing and less people believed in gods, instead, they believe more in scientific things than religious things. The gods were very angry of that, the wizards said,”This is the god’s final line, we should respect that, if thy have reached the climax, thy will done soon,” the wizards were been sentenced to jail because the bad prediction. As the wizard said, the gods use frozen airs, forest fire, floods, storms, and even hurricanes to destroy people’s building and their life, the “wizards” that were sentenced to jail released to fixed the problem, they made them to stop the researches and anything about science, and made the religious buildings build again, then, the gods were never done that divine punishment. Ever since then, human were afraid of gods, and their technology stay at the stage even before electricity were defined. A lot of cities like Baquran don’t have a lot of good equipment nor they have good technology in their own countries/layers. The people here were sad about the punishment, it bothered their own schedule and need to fix everywhere, the floods and the hurricanes destroyed almost everything in the world, the other beast saw human weaken, so they fight against human beings together as 8 species, the gods were confused again, so they created the past legends.

Past Legend

Past legends are things that were made by the time the big fight between the gods, they are actually just weapon that each god had at first, then they find out they are not able to use it because they are to powerful, so they send the legends to the 7 strongest species. And of course, each weapon represents a god, the whistle, which represents the hope god, the wand, which represents the kind god, the marble, which represents the friendship god, the infinite water bucket, which represents the sadness god, the drum, which represents the thankful god, a rose, which represents the beauty god, finally, a chair, which represents the calm chair, the whistle, which symbolize hope, the marble, made the god think of playing marble with friends, so it symbolizes friendship. The wand, which helps people that had problem with health and physical injure, which symbolizes kind of saving others’ life. The infinite bucket, represents infinite crying, which symbolizes sadness. The drum, when you play in the thanksgiving, which symbolizes thank, and the rose, which symbolizes beauty. And finally the chair, which let you calm when you sit on it, which represents calm.

Each legend is a theme of the god, or a message that they want their people know, it might be a lesson, or power, or even just a chair, it don’t directly represents the gods’ power, but it affects the people a lot.

About Baquran
