Living Layer - Last Dance

“We…. We didn’t die?” said Somayeh

“I feel a huge pain and I fainted to the ground”

“Aiden flied and save us using his rope but he still crush to the ground because the huge amount of weight,”

“What about him, is he still okay?” asked Somayeh

“His power is heal, so I think he is still okay” answered Ricky

“let’s go to his house and see”said Adrina

They went to the huge tree-house in the forest, beside their territory, they saw Aiden sit on his bed, looking at the forest, “Hello Mr.Aiden, are you still okay?” Ricky asked

“Yeah, I’m fine, just make sure you guys don’t fall to that beast again, okay?”

“Okay, but how can we pass the beast,”

Aiden gave them a wand, “If someone injured when your adventure starts, use this wand to help them, I won’t follow you guys every time but this wand can heal your friends”

“This is one of the legends!”

“Right, so it contains my heal power, even though it can’t use as offense but it’s for healing I should say. And I am pretty sure you know how to use a wand” Aiden said

“Yes, I can, thank you for the wand, we shaw start our adventure to the south”

They said goodbye to the old king and start their adventure to the south

They walked the same path and find the huge goat again, as the goat yelled and them and try to move them away, Adrina blow the whistle, Ricky jumped and use the sword to chop into the goat’s head, the goat suddenly fainted to the ground, Ricky almost killed the goat, Somayeh use the wand to heal the goat back and they ran away from the goat.

“Nice Somayeh, now we can go! Lets RUN” yelled Ricky, he saw the goat chasing them again, they ran as they can and jump over the cliff. They fall to the little cliff and they climbed over the cliff in order to escape from the goat.

“Finally, we ran away from him” Somayeh said.

“Yes and we are going to use that strategy again on the next one!” Ricky said.

“Yes, agree” Adrina nodded as she walk first to see if there is any monsters over their pass, since there is only one way to go to the south, they need to go in the way that most monster lived and attacked/defended

They saw their old friend: the fog, the miserably fog, the fog appeared little by little and block their eyesight, but Ricky quickly blew the whistle and the fog suddenly disappear.

“Gotcha!” Ricky laughed at the disappearing fog, “We’ve met you several times, this is not the last time we will defeat you, we will always defeat you.”

Ricky knew that the fog is scared of people that were “Aggressive” or people that is confident or totally feeling not scared of the fog.

So the fog disappeared and they keep moving on to the next monster, this time, a brand new monster.

It looked like a piece of fire ball that were resting (floating) in the air, with several hands(also fire) surrounding the fireball and throws hot lava to the kids, this as they thought a name: the fire element monster, as Ricky yelled a signal, he jumped into the air and try to chop the fire element, the fire element turned around to defense Ricky and Somayeh fired water and ice to the fire element and the fire element melt as fast as the light. “Yes！We defeated the fire element, it’s so easy, “ Somayeh use the wand to absorb the energy of the fire element and she can spawn an element now, with some of her power.(mana)

Then it’s like several monsters with different elements, the ice element, the thunder element, water element, and the wind element, etc.

The kids defeated a lot of the monsters and finally reached the final boss: the huge spider, with hundreds of eyes, hundreds of legs, 8more times bigger than the kids, it also have poisonous liquid that can kill human right away, the kids was wondering how to kill the spider, suddenly the spider first had a move:

The spider moved toward them and try to attack, it spilled the liquid all over the ground.”Be sure don’t touch the special liquid!” Adrina yelled as she shot a quick arrow to broke its eyes, but the spider still have hundreds and hundreds of eyes, because the spider killed animals that have Aiden’s power, it still contains healing power, which make it undead, or immortal. Ricky chopped hundreds of legs, but the legs grew back again as fast as the goat heal. They try to not kill the spider and try to escape to the gate first, but it didn’t work and the spider almost killed Ricky, Somayeh blew the whistle and the spider didn’t have and effects at all, “What?! The whistle can’t do anything?” Somayeh thought, as she spawned the elements to fight with the spider and they slipped under the spider quickly, “Quick!!” Somayeh whispered to Ricky, they ran quietly under the spider and they plugged the key to the gate, “Get in!” as they all jumped into the gate, the spider saw that and destroyed the gate angrily, fortunately, they didn’t die because of the big deconstruction, they were traveled to the next layer: Rich layer
