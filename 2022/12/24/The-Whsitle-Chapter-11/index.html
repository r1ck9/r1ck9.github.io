“We…. We didn’t die?” said Somayeh

“I feel a huge pain and I fainted to the ground”

“Aiden flied and save us using his rope but he still crush to the ground because the huge amount of weight,”

“What about him, is he still okay?” asked Somayeh

“His power is heal, so I think he is still okay” answered Ricky

“let’s go to his house and see”said Adrina

They went to the huge tree-house in the forest, beside their territory, they saw Aiden sit on his bed, looking at the forest, “Hello Mr.Aiden, are you still okay?” Ricky asked

“Yeah, I’m fine, just make sure you guys don’t fall to that beast again, okay?”

“Okay, but how can we pass the beast,”

Aiden gave them a wand, “If someone injured when your adventure starts, use this wand to help them, I won’t follow you guys every time but this wand can heal your friends”

“This is one of the legends!”

“Right, so it contains my heal power, even though it can’t use as offense but it’s for healing I should say. And I am pretty sure you know how to use a wand” Aiden said

“Yes, I can, thank you for the wand, we shaw start our adventure to the south”

They said goodbye to the old king and start their adventure to the south

They walked the same path and find the huge goat again, as the goat yelled and them and try to move them away, Adrina blow the whistle, Ricky jumped and use the sword to chop into the goat’s head, the goat suddenly fainted to the ground, Ricky almost killed the goat, Somayeh use the wand to heal the goat back and they ran away from the goat.
