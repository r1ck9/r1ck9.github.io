Nuno the arrogant king

Merchant layer is a layer that look like a dark place that happen weird things everyday, but the controler of this place can control time, he can make the time stop and make the time back, if he find out that there is a murder or stoling happening around this layer, he will turn the time back when he/she didn’t crime, and send them to another time place. He is Nuno, the ruler of Merchant layer, a tall man that has a big crown on his head, with golden and red cloths, Gold shoes that looks expensive, he look like a arrogant king that no one wanted to deal with. When you heard the sound of clock tik, tok sounding loud in the sky, it’s he turning up the time back and forth. It won’t cause other layers collapsed, each layer was a independent city and place.
