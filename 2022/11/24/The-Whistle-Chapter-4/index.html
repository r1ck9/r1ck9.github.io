The merchant layer-first view

Somayeh woke up slowly, “What happened? I remember we fell to a river,” said Somayeh.

“Do YOU know how to SWIM” Asked Adrina angrily.

“NO, I never know that we are going to rivers so I don’t know how to swim.”replied Somayeh awkwardly.

“Fine, we all have things that we are not good at, don’t blame each other.”said Ricky.

“Okay, SIR” said Adrina with an annoying voice.

“This layer doesn’t not look like what I think, let’s look the book Joe gave to us.”said Somayeh, as she open the book, some weird light flashed out, “ What is that?” said Somayeh,”Don’t care about it, Joe must’ve put some magic on it.” As Ricky read out loud.

  • Today, Tomorrow, and yesterday. “Merchant Layer, which contains a lot of merchants and people that are rich……”

“We already know that, flip a page!” said Adrina running out of patience.

Nuno the arrogant king

Merchant layer is a layer that look like a dark place that happen weird things everyday, but the controller of this place can control time, he can make the time stop and make the time back, if he find out that there is a murder happening around this layer, he will turn the time back when he/she didn’t crime, and send them to another time place. He is Nuno, the ruler of Merchant layer, a tall man that has a big crown on his head, with golden and red cloths, Gold shoes that looks expensive, he look like an arrogant king that no one wanted to deal with. When you heard the sound of clock tick, tock sounding loud in the sky, it’s he turning up the time back and forth. It won’t cause other layers collapsed, each layer was an independent city and place. BUT, if you want to open the gate to go to next layer, you need to asked Nuno for the magic key to open the big gate.

“Okay, we didn’t know that, there is a weird king that like to turn time and stop time,” said Ricky.

“Don’t talk, keep reading, there is a little story there,” as Somayeh pointed out:

Once upon a time… there is seven god that stand for their country, they are kind god, hope god, thankful god, friend god, beauty god, sadness god, and calm god. They all controlled a part of the old Baquran, which there is no layers at all, people just have big walls that border out the border for different “countries”. But, when they find out that religious people is decreasing and less people believed in gods, instead, they believe more in scientific things than religious things. The gods were very angry of that and they use frozen airs, forest fire, floods, storms, and even hurricanes to destroy people’s building and their life, the “wizards” that were sentenced to jail released to fixed the problem, they made them to stop the researches and anything about science, and made the religious buildings build again, then, the gods were never done that divine punishment.

“This book helped a lot, let me open a magical place to put those things.” As Somayeh said, she opened a big door and walked into it: It is a big place that you can put anything in there, she gently put the book down on a shelf, and then walked out to the real world.

“Somayeh? When did your magic becomes so good?” asked Adrina

“I practice a lot in my personal place, when in there, time also don’t work,” answered Somayeh.

“Let’s go find king Nuno.” Said Ricky shortly. They went to the place that most people hang out, and they asked a man,”Hi sir, do you know where the king live?…” The man pulled them to a place where no one was there,”Are you guys new to this layer? Why you ask about the king? You don’t know it is illegal? You are trying to leave here right? Go to this place on the map, there is the king live.”

“Thank You!”

“He must’ve been some kind a ‘criminal’ in the city, since he knows that much about the king and even where he live, by the way, why Nuno don’t want his citizen find their king’s palace?” Said Ricky.

“I don’t know, maybe he don’t want people killing him, even though he can turn the time,” replied Somayeh.

“Okay, stop arguing about the weird ‘criminal’ let’s find the palace!” Said Adrina.

They went all the way west to the almost near the edge of Baquran, where is the big ocean, called Melle Ocean, when they were in the way to the western Merchant layer, they were introduced to a special Organization: MPG(mysterious problem group) They were persuaded to join, because they think the kids were unique and the kids seems will help the MPG a lot. “MPG is a group of people that knows magic or fighting skills that find and solve problem in everywhere in Baquran, they have different territories in most layers except the living layer(because they can’t solve the animals always attacking them). Living layer, which is the next layer if you are planning to go to the center of Baquran. So, our request is you guys go to the living layer and build a building there that can stay and be our shelters,” said the leader of MPG, “oh, by the way, my name is Jake Bawa, you can call me Jake. Of course.”

“Wait, are you the son of Mr.Bawa who opened the magical shop?” asked Ricky.

“Yes, he is my dad, and he was the leader of MPG, but he got a horrible disease that make him can’t run, so he gave the opportunity of the leader to me,” replied Jake.
