Prepare and Armor

As Joe gave the map, and a book, they were not so ready to start the adventure, they need some prepare in armors and weapons. So they went to a shop that were high-lighted in the map: The Bawa shop.

“Hi, welcome to the Bawa shop, what do you want?” said the cashier that looked like a rich man.

“I want to buy some weapons, for me and my friends.” As Somayeh pointed to her friends.

“Uhh… Okay, you, I think you should get a magical wand. The boy, maybe a sword, and the girl, how about a bow, and arrows of course.”said the man with a persuasive tone.

“Okay, well, I’m fine with it, what about you guys?”Somayeh looked at they.

“I’m fine, it hears cool to be a warrior.” Said Ricky.

“Me too, I like to use bow and arrows.”Said Adrina.

“Also, I think you should get some cloths, the cloths you worn was too dirty and small for you.” Said the man.

“Oh yeah, we didn’t noticed that our cloths were dirty.”As Somayeh examine her poor shirt with dirts and wrinkles.

“I will be right back,” said the man.

As the man took several nice cloths out from his back closet, Somayeh put exactly 50 dollars on the table, well, half of the money Joe gave to her.

“Thank you Mr….”

“Mr.Bawa.”

“Thank you Mr.Bawa!” said Somayeh with thank.

“No problem, welcome you coming back when all of the mess ends,” said Mr.Bawa.

As they changed the cloths and picked up the weapons, a brand new group appeared: Somayeh with a magic hat, and cloths with stars twinkling on it, boots that adapted and fit in to the grounds when they walked. Adrina with a purple-blueish shirt, a quiver on her back, use for the arrows and with a bow on her hand. Also, a comfortable shoes that make her run fast and climbing very well. Ricky with a medal helmet, and a shiny armor protecting him from arrows or magic, or even hot and cold! They thought they’re ready now, and they opened the map that Joe gave to them:

They were at tech layer, which most academies and labs located. They want to go to the center of Baquran to get the special plant, and what they might first, was the merchant layer, which filled with people that trade things and buy things using the currency that was available in their special zones. Just like what happening on Earth: China got RMB, America got dollars($)… They find something in the book that Joe gave it to them: “If you want to cross between the layers, one way is to sit on the sky train which is located only in the center, or just go through the layer doors which allowed you travel between layers and layers.”

“Okay, so we need to find something called the ‘layer doors’ and go out of the tech layer?” Asked Adrina.

“Yes, I think we should go south since the map says the directions, I guess the door is exactly locate at the middle of the line between Merchant layer and us.” Replied Somayeh.

“Let’s go south and try to find it, if we can’t, the only way is open that box.” Said Ricky.

So, they went south and try to find something, as they almost reached the layer door, suddenly, fogs came out and blind their sight. A mysterious sound came from the fogs.

“You trespassers, how you dare to come to my place, are you going to cross the door? You know that is hard right?”

She grabbed Ricky and Adrina away using her tentacles.

“Yes, Ms.fog? can you let us go to the door, please and thank you,” said Somayeh gently.

“You can’t pass here only when you figured out 3 riddles, for you, or else, you won’t get back. I will make your friends as my lunch, since I am hungry now.” laughed the fog.

“Okay, say the riddles” said Somayeh.

“What is a room you can’t live in? you have 3 guesses”

“Hum…. first guess: a locked room? “

“Nope.”

“A b-room(broom)?”

“Nope, it need to be a word“

“A mushroom!”

“HOW DID YOU GOT IT!” Said the fog with sad and angry.

“Here’s the second one: what grows more when you take more from it?”

“A hole! HA I know this one for sure,” said Somayeh.

“You got it again!”

“Okay, the final one, The more there is, the less you see. What am I?”

“Hum… knowledge?”

“Close, but not, two guesses left!”

“Oh, darkness, the more there is, the less you see.”

“Ahhhh, you got it,” said the fog sadly.

The fog disappeared slowly and put Ricky and Adrina down.

Adrina looked so scared that a cute rabbit could even cause a heart attack.

“Okay, we finally safe now. So that is the door, we can now go easily.”said Ricky calmly, they went to the door slowly, with alert, they checked that things happening around them, and be sure that they were super safe, they went to the door. The door was super big, and the temperature around them was low, the door looked like a transporting machine, they carefully checked around the huge door, and Ricky found a note: “Be alert, don’t trust anyone except yourself.” They shared the information they found, there was a thing that looked like a open of the machine, that might open the gate, Ricky gave the note to them, and looked at other friends with a strange way,”Just like what I think, there will be magic monsters that might be a clone of us.” Said him with a heavy voice,”but, what I think only might help us is Somayeh’s magic, maybe it will clear the effects on monsters,” They thought they were ready to press the open button, so Adrina went in front of it and press the button, “Splash”. As the weird gate appeared the sound, and then they were squished to the gate(the button magically turned off by itself).

Somayeh yelled unclearly,”Why are we in…. a river … soaking … wet?”

“I…don’t…know!”

“Go on the land!”

They swam slowly to the land, as Ricky first reached the land, he looked at Somayeh whose almost drowning in the water. He didn’t hesitate at all, he jumped into the water and swam as fast as a fish toward Somayeh. As he looked at Somayeh closed her eyes and fell into the water, he quickly grabbed her, took her back to the land as fast as he can. He finally put his breath down, when he put her on the land. Adrina knew a bit rescue thing, and she done the CPR for Somayeh and finally made her back from the death lord.
