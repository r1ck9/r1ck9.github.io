Prepare and Armor

As Joe gave the map, and the box, they were not so ready to start the adventure, they need some prepare in armors and weapons. So they went to a shop that were high-lighted in the map: The Bawa shop.

“Hi, welcome to the Bawa shop, what do you want?” said the cashier that looked like a rich man.

“I want to buy some weapons, for me and my friends.” As Somayeh pointed to her friends.

“Uhh… Okay, you, I think you should get a magical wand. The boy, maybe a sword, and the girl, how about a bow, and arrows of course.”said the man with a persuasive tone.

“Okay, well, I’m fine with it, what about you guys?”Somayeh looked at they.

“I’m fine, it hears cool to be a warrior.” Said Ricky.

“Me too, I like to use bow and arrows.”Said Adrina.

“Also, I think you should get some cloths, the cloths you worn was too dirty and small for you.” Said the man.

“Oh yeah, we didn’t noticed that our cloths were dirty.”As Somayeh examine her poor shirt with dirts and wrinkles.

“I will be right back,” said the man.

As the man took several nice cloths out from his back closet, Somayeh put exactly 50 dollars on the table, well, half of the money Joe gave to her.

“Thank you Mr….”

“Mr.Bawa.”

“Thank you Mr.Bawa!” said Somayeh with thank.

“No problem, welcome you coming back when all of the mess ends,” said Mr.Bawa.

As they changed the cloths and picked up the weapons, a brand new group appeared: Somayeh with a magic hat, and cloths with stars twinkling on it, boots that adapted and fit in to the grounds when they walked. Adrina with a purple-blueish shirt, a quiver on her back, use for the arrows and with a bow on her hand. Also, a comfortable shoes that make her run fast and climbing very well.
