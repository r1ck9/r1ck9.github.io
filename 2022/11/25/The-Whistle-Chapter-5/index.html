Merchant Layer - Going Deeper

As they farewelled the MPG, they decided to go straight to the palace, whoever called them, they will not stop. But, before the rush, they need to rest for a minute, they don’t want to loose time, so they went to Somayeh’s personal space, “I want to give a name to this space, since we learned and read in here, how ‘bout call it classroom. “ said Somayeh.

“It’s your space, you decide, by the way, I think it’s cool, “ said Adrina. Ricky also nodded to agree.

“Okay, let’s look at the book again and see if there is more information,” said Ricky.

“OK”

“OK”

As Ricky opened the book, the weird light pop out again, this time, not so bright as last time, but this weird green light made them confused.

“This weird light appeared again,” said Somayeh.

“It’s just Magic and just read,” said Adrina.

“Adrina, why you are so angry these days,” asked Somayeh,

“You, and also you!” As Adrina pointed to Ricky.

“You two always put the important thing on his or yours shoulders, and I never have the responsibility to keep something nor the opportunity to do something, if there is someone writing a story for us, they will just write,Ricky and Somayeh, not ADRINA!” said Adrina angrily,”You guys forget I’m the sister of Marie, I’m the one the most shocked and dismay, why you and you seems as sad as a bird who lost it’s mom, I’m the one who always sad, these days I’m extremely sad because you two, you guys even don’t care a thing for me, no one said a word like are you okay like the time I’m also in the water, I’m also near drowned, but no one save me, I use my magic to pull me up in order to survive, you don’t even know what I experienced and what I like or not, you just randomly give jobs that I don’t like, I’m way sad than you two, and you two make me even more sad than before,” said Adrina emotionally.

“Okay, we are sorry, I really don’t know you are sister of Marie, and I really didn’t notice you are almost drowned, I see you swim toward the land, so I go to save Somayeh, I’m really sorry, I know you don’t like talk with people that you are not familiar with, you don’t like to talk with little kids or big kids, apparently, you just don’t like to talk with anyone,” said Ricky.

“Thanks fo your help, I feel like I’m better now,” said Adrina.

“Now try to talk more and don’t be shy, people always a little shy when they met new people that they don’t know,” said Ricky.

“You can sleep here if you want, you seem tired enough of the thing now,” said Somayeh with deep concern.

As Adrina slept down, she had a dream again:

She was in that big house again and she open the window and the door, animals like birds, insects all appeared in the big big forest, and also her friends, Ricky and Somayeh helped to open several windows, and also the doors that was not only the front door, and she was ‘bothered’ now, but she feels happy enough.
