Introduction

Somayeh is a typical grade 7 student, who is currently learning in Maywood. She has a lot of friends and she likes going to school. On one unusual day, she, as usual, went back to her house when the school ends. As she walked back, suddenly, she fell into a big hole from nowhere. Her yell vanished in the world, she also vanished. No-one noticed that at all, because that day no-one was in the way Somayeh went. However, she stood up from the hole, looked exactly fine, not even seemed injured at all. But thats not Somayeh, that was a clone of Somayeh, someone pretended to be her. The true Somayeh was transported into a brand new world that she never knew . . . .

The fake Somayeh pretended to be the true and went back to her home without any changes, if Somayeh’s friend come and see her, they might can’t even notice that it is not the true one.She did what Somayeh did, she lived as a shadow of Somayeh, mysterious and miserable. As someone else appeared in her room as not be noticed by her mom, they opened a gate, or practically a hole, with ice and flame, white and black. The hole appeared big in Somayeh’s small room, but it was actually small.The someone else said,”l’m going to Baquran, I’m not wasting time here!”as he jumped into the big gate, the gate was twinkling like a star. Beautifully, as it was, made him to a place we are familiar: the sky train station, but for a different job to do.”Thank you for coming to the sky train-Metrotown station, next train:to Baquran coming in 5minutes.”said from the above.As Joe waited for the train, he was thinking about some important things: about researches and academies he opened at a lot of places.

The sky train came quickly, as fast as a cheetah. The people all moved quickly, so as Joe, he got on the train and start waiting for the final station: Baquran.

“Next station:Baquran, doors will open at the left, final station alert!”said from the system again, Joe noticed that.”beep”,as the sky train reached the station, everyone went down like a crowd of little bees searching for honey from the flower.Joe got out and moved away to the city, as he examined the world, he was actually shocked: red moon suspended in the dark sky, died plants eating by black goats, the whole city looked lifeless and mysterious.”I just didn’t come here for 1 year and Baquran changed so much!”said Joe with surprise.

Baquran was a peaceful city before, everyone here was a magician of at least know magic, everyone here had the opportunity to learn magic in special schools, however,Joe was different, he couldn’t use or learn magic at all, no one knew why, his family abandoned him because of that, because magic was important in Baquran. A lot of things you need magic to do and often teachers were chose by the quality and the quantity of their magic and knowledge.

Baquran has several layers in it, so it is hard to control the information happening currently all around the whole city.So people created CAB(curiosity about Baquran) for people to know the information about everywhere quickly and informative. Joe, was the leader of CAB.A lot of young people joined CAB because they just curious about everything, that was normal happening between the youth. The CAB often put people into experiments and tests to continue in their technology research.

Somayeh, one of the human beings that was pulled here to do experiments that help their research. They’ve been to the lab, everyone whose doing experiments here have a special, unique number.For an example, Somayeh’s number is “#370124”.

As they went to the center of the academy, Somayeh asked some questions:”Where are we going, what are we doing, why are we here?”.

Joe replied with patience, “We are going to the centre for a shot, you are going to take an experiment,I will tell you the consequences of it, the effects of it, the rewards of it later when we reached okay?”

Somayeh replied,”Yes sir!”, that made some laugh with the adults in the line whose number with #2324, #3444, and #1322.

(They are much before me) Somayeh thought.

As they reached the centre of the academy, Joe gave a powerful, informative, persuasive speech and excellent explanation:”The injection is basically a test that send a special chemical to you nerve function system, and made your nerve send back another signal to tell us about if we can turn the monsters back to human beings, the effects might be that your brain got tired of that vibration in your nerve and declined to be send a message from our academy, that is fine, no worries about that. Finally, when you finish all of the test part, you will get 100 dollars for rewards.”
