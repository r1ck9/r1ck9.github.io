Past Legends, The God

Past legends are things that were made by the time the big fight between the gods, they are actually just weapon that each god had at first, then they find out they are not able to use it because they are to powerful, so they send the legends to the 7 strongest species. And of course, each weapon represents a god, the whistle, which represents the hope god, the wand, which represents the kind god, the marble, which represents the friendship god, the infinite water bucket, which represents the sadness god, the drum, which represents the thankful god, a rose, which represents the beauty god, finally, a chair, which represents the calm chair, the whistle, which symbolize hope, the marble, made the god think of playing marble with friends, so it symbolizes friendship. The wand, which helps people that had problem with health and physical injure, which symbolizes kind of saving others’ life. The infinite bucket, represents infinite crying, which symbolizes sadness. The drum, when you play in the thanksgiving, which symbolizes thank, and the rose, which symbolizes beauty. And finally the chair, which let you calm when you sit on it, which represents calm.

Each legend is a theme of the god, or a message that they want their people know, it might be a lesson, or power, or even just a chair, it don’t directly represents the gods’ power, but it affects the people a lot.
